# **Product Requirements Document (PRD)**

## **Platform: Hum-Awaaz Official Web Portal**

### **1\. Product Vision**

To provide local Union Committee (UC) officials in Karachi with a centralized, data-driven web dashboard to manage, prioritize, and resolve citizen complaints while rebuilding public trust through visual proof of work.

### **2\. Target Audience**

Union Committee Chairmen, Vice Chairmen, UC Secretaries, and administrative staff managing neighborhood operations.

### **3\. Core Features**

* **Executive Metrics Dashboard:** A high-level analytics view showing total reports received, pending tasks, acknowledged issues, and successfully resolved cases. Calculates an automatic "Resolution Rate" (Trust Score).  
* **Urgency-Based Triage System:** A ticketing queue where citizen complaints are automatically sorted by the number of "Upvotes" they have received, ensuring officials see the most pressing neighborhood issues first.  
* **Departmental Assignment:** Officials can review a complaint and assign it to a specific civic department (e.g., Solid Waste Management, Water Board, Roads & Works) to route the issue effectively.  
* **Status Management & Public Remarks:** Officials can change ticket statuses (Pending, Acknowledged, Resolved) and are required to add public text remarks explaining the action taken (e.g., "Team dispatched for inspection").  
* **"Success Photo" Proof System:** To close a ticket and mark it "Resolved," officials must upload a photo of the completed work. This photo is immediately visible to the public.  
* **Broadcast Engine:** Officials can publish two types of notices to the Resident App:  
  1. *General Announcements* (e.g., Anti-dengue fumigation schedules).  
  2. *Civic Cooperation Requests* (e.g., "Please remove parked cars from Street 5 for sweeping").  
* **Office Settings Management:** Officials can toggle their office status (Open/Closed) in real-time, which instantly updates on the resident mobile app to prevent wasted physical trips.
