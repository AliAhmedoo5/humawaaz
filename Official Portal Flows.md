# **User Flows**

## **Platform: Hum-Awaaz Official Web Portal**

### **1\. Secure Login & Triage Flow**

1. UC Official navigates to the web portal URL.  
2. Logs in using admin-provisioned credentials.  
3. Lands on the Executive Dashboard. Reviews top-level metrics (Resolution Rate).  
4. Navigates to the "Requires Immediate Attention" queue to view highly upvoted complaints.

### **2\. Ticket Management Flow (Acknowledgment)**

1. Official clicks on a newly reported issue.  
2. Reviews the resident's photo, description, and map location.  
3. Selects an appropriate department from the dropdown (e.g., "Water Board").  
4. Changes status to "Acknowledged".  
5. Types a public remark: "We have contacted the water board for inspection."  
6. Clicks Submit. The resident app feed updates in real-time.

### **3\. Resolution & Proof Flow**

1. Physical repair work is completed in the neighborhood.  
2. Official opens the portal and finds the acknowledged ticket.  
3. Changes status to "Resolved".  
4. System prompts for a "Success Photo".  
5. Official uploads the photo of the repaired asset.  
6. Clicks Submit. The ticket is closed, and metrics are updated.

### **4\. Broadcasting Flow**

1. Official navigates to the "Notice Board" tab.  
2. Clicks "New Broadcast".  
3. Selects broadcast type: "Announcement" or "Civic Request".  
4. Drafts a subject and message (e.g., "Fumigation drive tomorrow at 5 PM").  
5. Clicks Publish. The notice appears instantly on all resident apps within that UC.
