# **Product Requirements Document (PRD)**

## **Platform: Hum-Awaaz Resident Mobile App**

### **1\. Product Vision**

To provide a secure, verified, and transparent mobile platform for the citizens of Karachi to report civic issues, engage with their local Union Committee (UC), and hold local officials accountable through public visibility.

### **2\. Target Audience**

Citizens and residents of Karachi (across 246+ UCs) who own a smartphone and require a direct channel to report neighborhood infrastructure and maintenance issues.

### **3\. Core Features**

* **Geofenced Auto-Assignment:** Users drop a pin on a map during registration. The system automatically calculates which UC boundary they fall into (e.g., UC-5 Safoora) and locks their account to that area.  
* **On-Device OCR Verification (The Trust Gate):** To prevent spam, users must scan a K-Electric bill, SSGC bill, or the back of their CNIC. The app extracts the address locally on the device to verify residency. Unverified users can only view the feed; verified users can post.  
* **Photo-First Complaint Reporting:** Users are required to upload a live photo or gallery image of the civic issue (e.g., burst pipe, overflowing trash). Text-only complaints are not allowed to ensure data quality.  
* **Community Validation (Upvoting):** Residents can "Upvote" or "Verify" existing issues reported by neighbors. This prevents duplicate tickets and helps officials gauge the urgency of an issue.  
* **Two-Way Transparency Timeline:** Residents can view a public timeline of official actions on any complaint (e.g., "Pending" \-\> "Acknowledged" \-\> "Resolved").  
* **Public Notice Board:** A dedicated tab for residents to read official announcements, water schedules, and civic cooperation requests directly from their UC Chairman.  
* **UC Information Hub:** A public profile of the UC showing the Chairman's name, official contact number, current office status (Open/Closed), and public dealing timings.
